How does it work?

You're covered for returns on all Ulike devices. Just a few things to keep in mind:
The device must be purchased from our official website.
We can't accept returns if the product is damaged or stained (think scratches, marks, or signs of heavy use).
Make sure all original items are included: the device, accessories, manual, cables, and packaging.

Returning is easy

Once we've received and checked your return, we'll issue your refund to the original payment method within 3–7 business days.
