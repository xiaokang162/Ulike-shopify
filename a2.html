Is IPL Right for You

The effectiveness of IPL hair removal is based on the ratio between melanin in the skin and hair. It is recommended for dark hair and lighter skin tones, in order for the treatment to be effective and safe.

How IPL Works

IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) is a hair removal method that targets the melanin inside hair follicles, utilizing a powerful and safe spectrum of light. This results in visible hair reduction without damaging the skin.
