Alaska, Hawaii & U.S. Territories
We ship orders to U.S. states and territories, including Hawaii, Alaska, Puerto Rico, APO/FPO addresses, and PO boxes.

Pricing and estimated delivery times are based on the destination and are shown at checkout. Orders are shipped via United States Postal Service (USPS). Delivery to Alaska & Hawaii generally takes 8-21 business days.

Risk and Loss: All items purchased from us are subject to a shipment contract. This implies that the risk of loss and title for such items transfers to the customer upon our delivery to the carrier.

Claims Processing: Ulike monitors and logs all reports of lost, missing, incorrect, or damaged items. Lost, missing, or wrong/damaged items must be reported within 7 calendar days of the order being marked as delivered. For wrong/damaged items, accompanying pictures must be submitted. Ulike retains the right to restrict and/or reject the reshipment and/or refunding of an order/item in all circumstances. In cases where a courtesy reshipment is issued, the reshipment order is not eligible for return and/or refund.
